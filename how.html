How

For digital phenotyping to benefit people, it is important to integrate it with clinical practice. In this way psychiatrists can treat mental illness much earlier.

Realizing Digital Phenotyping

Bringing the possible benefits of digital phenotyping into reality requires careful consideration and cooperation from many different disciplines. Based on the current progress in digital phenotyping research, the end goal is in sight. 

Steps

There are three key steps that will allow digital phenotyping research to get to a place where clinicians can feel confident in using it to treat patients.

1

Create shared data collection platforms and repositories

Shared platforms will accelerate digital phenotyping research. It will give more researchers the ability to create more reliable studies. 
2

Address ethical concerns

Digital phenotyping turns data into sensitive health information. It is incredibly important to make sure this data is secure and not used for anything but helping the patient.
3

Partner with technology companies

The last piece of the puzzle is harnessing the incredible innovation and technology that technology companies can provide. Digital phenotyping requires cutting edge data science techniques.
